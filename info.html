Cuneiform-WebGLViewer

This viewer for web-based 3D visualization of cuneiform data, hereafter referred to as Cuneiform-WebGLViewer (more information at webglviewer.cuneiform.de), was coded 2017 by Denis Fisseler in the scope of his work as a research assistant at TU Dortmund University, Department of Computer Science VII. This work was part of the research project "Web-basierte Analyse von 3D Keilschriftdaten", supervised by Frank Weichert with financial participation of the TU Dortmund University, the University of Würzburg and the Gisela and Reinhold Häcker Foundation.
